What is the Box Model in CSS?

  • The CSS Box Model consists of Content, Padding, Border, and Margin.
  • Content — The actual content like text or image.
  • Padding — Space between content and border. It’s transparent.
  • Border — The edge that wraps padding and content.
  • Margin — Space outside the border. It separates elements.
  • To include padding and border in total size, use box-sizing: border-box;
